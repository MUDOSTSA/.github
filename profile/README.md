# Mapúa University DOST Scholars Association (MU-DOST SA)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **Mapúa University DOST Scholars Association (MU-DOST SA)**.
This serves as the central repository for all digital systems, research outputs, and software initiatives created and maintained by the organization and its members.

---

## Purpose

This organization exists to:

* Centralize all technical and digital projects developed under MU-DOST SA.
* Promote collaboration, transparency, and innovation among DOST scholars.
* Preserve institutional knowledge by documenting and version-controlling organizational systems.
* Support future scholars through shared tools, resources, and codebases.

---
